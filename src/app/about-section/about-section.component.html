Logbook


Logbook is an application that is being used for tracking the progress of the students.


It was created in 2021. The logbook facilitates the work of teachers and students.


    Application have three parts :
  • Admin
  • Professor
  • Student

    • Admin have a role :
  • Add new user
  • List of student
  • List of professor

    • Student have a role :
  • Add new patients.
  • Student already have available list of examined patients.

    • Professor :
  • Role of the professor is to evaluate student when he examines every patient.
